How Predictions are Made

This tool is powered by a Design of Experiments (DOE) model. A DOE study was run in ANSYS to test many combinations of geometry and forces. This calculator uses that comprehensive data to provide instant predictions, acting as a user-friendly dashboard for the complex simulation results.
